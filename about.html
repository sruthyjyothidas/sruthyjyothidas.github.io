👋 About Me

I am an experimental nuclear physicist exploring the behavior of matter under extreme conditions, recreating the first moments of the universe in laboratory experiments.

My research focuses on studying Quark-Gluon Plasma (QGP), a state of matter where quarks and gluons move freely, produced in high-energy collisions at facilities like CERN and Brookhaven National Laboratory.

I am particularly interested in photonuclear collisions and understanding how QGP-like signals emerge even in small systems.

🔬 Research Interests

  • Quark-Gluon Plasma (QGP)
  • Photonuclear collisions
  • Small system collectivity
  • Heavy-ion physics
